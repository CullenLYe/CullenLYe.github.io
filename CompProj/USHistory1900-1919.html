1900-1919

Presidents During This Period:
1896-1901: William McKinley
1901-1908: Theodore Roosevelt
1908-1912: William Howard Taft
1912-1920: Woodrow Wilson


Notable Events:
1902: The Philippine-American War ends.
1903: The Hay-Herran Treaty allows the United States to begin construction of the Panama Canal.
1903: The first silent film is released.
1903: The Wright brothers fly the first airplane.
1906: The Great San Francisco Earthquake hits California, killing thousands.
1907: Pablo Picassp creates Cubism, a new art style.
1909: The National Association for the Advancement of Colored People (NAACP) is founded.
1910: The Boy Scouts of America is founded.
1912: The RMS Titanic sinks after hitting an iceberg.
1914: The Panama Canal is finished.
1914: The Jones-Shafroth Act is signed, giving U.S. citizenship to Puerto Ricans.
1915: The Lusitania is sunk by the Germans, angering the American public.
1917: America joins World War 1.
