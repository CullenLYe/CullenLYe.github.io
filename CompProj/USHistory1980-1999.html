1980-1999

Presidents During This Period:
1976-1980: Jimmy Carter
1980-1988: Ronald Reagan
1988-1992: George H. W. Bush
1992-2000: Bill Clinton


Notable Events:
1980: America boycotts the Summer Olympics in Moscow to protest the Soviet Union's invasion of Afghanistan.
1980: Mount Saint Helens, a volcano in Washington, erupts.
1980: John Lennon, a member of The Beatles, is assassinated.
1981: Sandra Day O'Connor becomes the first woman Supreme Court justice.
1983: Sally Ride becomes the first American woman to go to space.
1986: The Challenger, a U.S. space shuttle, explodes after liftoff, killing the astronauts.
1987: In an event later called Black Monday, the stock market significantly crashed.
1991: The Cold War ends as the Berlin Wall is torn down and the Soviet Union collapses.
1993: The Oslo Accords attempts to mend relations in the Middle East, between Israel and Palestine.
1998: Google is founded.
1998: President Bill Clinton becomes the first U.S. President to be impeached, for perjury and obstruction of justice.
