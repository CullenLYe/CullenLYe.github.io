2000-2019

Presidents During This Period:
1992-2000: Bill Clinton
2000-2008: George W. Bush
2008-2016: Barack Obama
2016-2020: Donald J. Trump


Notable Events:
2001: Al-Qaeda coordinated attacks on the World Trade Center, Pentagon, and Washington D.C., resulting in thousands of casualties.
2001: The United States began its War on Terror.
2001: United States invades Afghanistan, in an attempt to overthrow al-Qaeda. This war is still ongoing.
2001: The controversial USA PATRIOT Act is passed, increasing the government's power to monitor communications.
2002: The Department of Homeland Security is formed in response to the September 11 Attacks.
2003: America invades Iraq, in an attempt to overthrow Saddam Hussein, starting the Iraq War.
2005: YouTube is founded.
2005: Hurricane Katrina hits Louisiana.
2006: North Korea succesffuly conducted its first nuclear test.
2007: The first iPhone is released.
2007: A financial crisis, caused by the unpopularity of President Bush and the Iraq war, hurts America's economy. Many say it's the worst financial crisis since the Great Depression.
2008: Barack Obama, the first black president, is elected president.
2010: Obama announces the end of the War on Terror, which doesn't end up happening.
2011: U.S. Navy SEALs kill Osama bin Laden, al-Qaeda's leader.
2013: The Boston Marathon Bombing takes place, in which people were killed near the Boston Marathon's finish line.
2017: After a very controversial election, Donald J. Trump is inaugurated as the U.S. President.
