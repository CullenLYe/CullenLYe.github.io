The Number Game

The website will think of a random number from 1 to 999, inclusive. Your goal is to guess it.
Input a number and the computer will tell you if it's too high or too low.
Also, it will remember your previous guesses and help you narrow down your choices.
As you would expect, if you get the number, you win!
Try to win with a small number of guesses. Don't worry, it keeps track for you.
