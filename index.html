A Quick Intro...

Hello! I'm Cullen Ye.
  • I'm currently pursuing a Master of Divinity with a concentration in Apologetics and Philosophy at The Southern Baptist Theological Seminary, expecting to graduate in 2026.
  • I serve as the Pastoral Intern at Brooklyn Community Christian Church.
  • I also serve as a Seekers Brooklyn Campus Staff for Urban Youth Alliance International.
  • I graduated summa cum laude from The University of Michigan in 2024, with a BSE in Computer Science and a minor in Philosophy.
    • In college, I was a Student Leader at Anchor College Church, a member of the UMich Powerlifting Club, and a Blue Market Employee.
  • I'm from Brooklyn, New York, and I graduated from Stuyvesant High School in 2021.
    • In high school, I was Vice President of Stuyvesant Christian Seekers, in Stuyvesant ARISTA, and on the Stuy Math Team.
  • Here are some of my hobbies/interests...
    • Studies: Philosophy, Theology, Apologetics
    • Sports: Powerlifting, Table Tennis
    • Instruments: Guitar, Ukelele, Cajón
    • Other: Reading, Nature, Poetry
