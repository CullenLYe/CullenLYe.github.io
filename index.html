A Quick Intro...

Hello! I'm Cullen Ye.
  • I'm pursuing a Master of Divinity with concentrations in Apologetics, Philosophy, and Biblical Theology at The Southern Baptist Theological Seminary, expecting to graduate in December 2026.
  • I serve as the Pastoral Intern at Brooklyn Community Christian Church in Brooklyn, New York.
  • I also serve as Seekers Brooklyn Campus Staff for Urban Youth Alliance International.
  • I graduated summa cum laude from The University of Michigan in 2024, with a BSE in Computer Science and a minor in Philosophy.
  • Outside of ministry, I love powerlifting, playing table tennis, strumming my guitar, and watching college football!
